What to Prepare Before Contacting Us

  • Your business objective and target markets, especially Europe or North America.
  • The product type: website, mobile app, management system, or integrated digital service.
  • Expected launch channels such as Google Play, Apple App Store, or web distribution.
  • Whether you need advertising monetization, data processing, marketing planning, or system integration.
  • Preferred timeline, major dependencies, and any known compliance constraints.

Providing these details early allows us to prepare a faster and more accurate project discovery discussion.
